Column Reasoning
- **Intern_ID**: Unique identifier for each intern
- **Name**: Intern full name
- **Department**: UI/UX, Data Science, Development
- **Tasks_Assigned**: Total tasks assigned per week
- **Tasks_Completed**: Tasks actually completed by the intern
- **Attendance**: Attendance percentage of intern
- **Feedback**: Mentor rating (numeric, out of 5)
- **Submission_Timeliness**: Score representing on-time submission
- **Week**: Week number for tracking intern progress
